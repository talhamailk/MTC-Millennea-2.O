Code of Conduct

  • 1. The event management has full authority to expel any delegate or delegation from the event at any time for not complying with the event rules and/or code of conduct.
  • 2. Participants are strictly required to wear the participant tags issued by "MTC Millennia's Management". In case any participant loses their tag, they must report to the Registration heads immediately, upon which a new tag will be issued, at the cost of Pkr-250/-. The possession of tag is necessary for entering and exiting the school premises.
  • 3. In case of vandalizing school property, appropriate penalty will be charged.
  • 4. Use of foul language and infliction of violence towards the MTC Millennia's management or any delegate will not be tolerated and will result in immediate disqualification and expulsion.
  • 5. Use of any illegal items and/or intoxicants is strictly prohibited within the school premises. Strict action will be taken against those possessing any such items.
  • 6. All participants will be responsible for their own belongings.
  • 7. All delegations must be accompanied by a teacher.

Rules & Regulations

  • Only students up to O-Levels and Matric are allowed.
  • Institution registrations will be accepted on a first come, first-served basis.
  • Matches will strictly adhere to the pre-announced schedule, and no delays will be tolerated for any team.
  • All players must wear the appropriate sports uniform representing their respective institutions during matches.
  • * The Sports Department and the Executive Council of MTC Millennia reserve the right to cancel the registration of any team or disqualify a team during a match for violating the event's rules or code of conduct.
  • Teams must complete their registration formalities before they are allowed to participate in a match.
  • * MTC will provide on-site first aid facilities for players, but it shall not be held responsible for any serious injuries sustained by players.
  • •The referees' decisions shall be considered final.
  • •You are required to give the campus your smart card/b form.
  • •P.E teachers or the head delegate is requested to provide the list of their delegates with the categories they are participating in before the event.
