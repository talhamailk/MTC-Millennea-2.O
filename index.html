About Us

MTCMILLENNIA2.0 is a highly anticipated two-day sports event that brings together students, families, and the local community to celebrate athletic talent and school spirit. This year, we are expecting participation from over 270 students. The event will feature a range of athletic competitions and aims to promote teamwork, sportsmanship, and healthy living among our students. We are committed to making this event a memorable experience for everyone involved and believe that you can play a significant role in its success.

Head Delegate Form

The form has been attached below and can also be viewed online.

  • The team Leader form must be signed by the Institute’s Principal, Administrator, Guardian or Co-Curricular Department. This form will also need to be stamped with the official institute’s stamp.
  • The purpose of this form is to specify one student from your institute who the management will liaise with for matters of registration and will be considered team leader for the institute’s entire delegation (also known as the head delegate).
  • Each delegation will be led by a head delegate. All head delegates are required to fill the registration forms (head delegate form and delegation registration form)
  • The deadline for sending the above-mentioned form is the 13th of Octuber 2024.
